Altstore

  • If your on iOS 16, Read below.
  • To use AltStore on iOS 16 or later, you need to enable Developer Mode on your device by going to Settings -> Privacy and Security -> Developer Mode.
  • Download AltServer for Windows from altstore.io.
  • Extract AltInstaller.zip and run Setup.exe.
  • Go to search in your Windows task bar, then type in "AltServer" and click "Run as administrator"
  • Connect your iOS device to your computer and make sure it is unlocked.
  • Open iTunes, then enable "Wi-Fi sync" on your device.
  • Click the AltServer icon in the bottom right taskbar.
  • Then go to “Install AltStore” and choose your iOS device.
  • Enter your Apple ID email address and password.
  • Wait a few seconds, then an AltServer notification should appear saying AltStore has been successfully installed onto your device.
  • On your iOS device, go to Settings -> General -> Profiles & Device Management.
  • Tap “Trust [Your Apple ID],” then tap “Trust” again to confirm.
